<p>The Hockey CCA aims to nurture students' interest and passion for the
game of Hockey as they learn the basic skills and rules of the sport.</p>
<p>In the course of the CCA, members will learn the values of resilience,
sportsmanship, respect, perseverance and teamwork.</p>
