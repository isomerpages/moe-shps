<p>The Table Tennis CCA aims to promote the sport to our students and develop
their skills in the sport. We aspire to give our players the opportunities
to take part in competitions. Holistically, we aim to help our students
develop a life-long interest in Table-Tennis and develop their psychological,
physiological and emotional skills during their time in the CCA.</p>
