<p>The Dance CCA aims to teach students about freedom of movement, allowing
them to freely express their style and innermost feelings boldly on stage
or in the presence of an audience. Through dance, the values of group work,
mutual respect, tolerance and continuous progress towards perfection are
inculcated.</p>
