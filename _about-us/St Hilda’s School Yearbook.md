SHPS Yearbook 
--------------------------------


Since 2021, in line with our commitment to environmental sustainability, we have decided to go green with our yearbook.

The publication will be available as a digital flipbook, accessible to all students free of charge. The physical yearbook will not be available for sale.

Click on the link below to view the publication. You will need to enter a password to access the content.

Please click 
[here](https://go.gov.sg/shps-yearbook)
for online viewing.

**Important Reminder:**

In compliance with the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and to protect the identity of our students, we kindly request that you do not share the password with individuals outside our school community. Your cooperation in safeguarding our students' personal information is greatly appreciated.
